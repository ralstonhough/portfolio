Name That Movie!

A little quiz game that feeds the player movie quotes over the course of about a minute. The player has to guess in which movies the quotes appear. The game lets the player know if they got it wrong and provides a final score at the end. Quotes do not repeat within a single game. The design is meant to evoke the aesthetics of the Golden Age of Hollywood.
