First Things First,
Second, and Third

Design and layout for a booklet promoting a hypo­thetical AIGA event on the legacy of the famous “First Things First” manifesto and its successors. Since the manifestos simultaneously provide a warning against the commercialization of the design discipline and a direction for its future, I drew on street signage as a visual inspiration.
