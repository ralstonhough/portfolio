“Offensive War is Murder”

A 16”x22” poster condemning Russia’s 2022 invasion of Ukraine. Disturbed by attempts to rationalize or excuse Russia's actions, I chose a slogan that presents the moral truth of unilateral warfare in stark terms to counteract misguided appeals to realpolitik or tu quoque arguments. The poster takes aesthetic and design cues from early 20th-century Russian Constructivist work. The typeface, Korolev, is inspired by Cyrillic forms seen in period military propaganda.
