Sanctuary

I made these pixelated, blackletter-inspired letter­forms for an exercise in CSS flexboxes. They're neat, so I am in the process of expanding them into a brand concept. The idea is that “Sanctuary” is a non-profit that provides communications access and security to persecuted religious groups. I might also expand the letters into a complete alphabet. In the meantime, I have adopted the “r” as a kind of personal symbol, such as in the favicon for this site.
